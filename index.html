About Us

“Built on instinct. Refined by intention.”

  • Where We Began

    Bravyn was born from a simple belief — that brand and creator collaborations should feel real, not forced. We saw too many campaigns chasing trends and missing the mark. So we set out to build something different: an agency rooted in creative clarity, honest results, and cultural relevance.

  • What We Do Differently

    We’re not here to scale fast and lose soul. We’re a small, hands-on team that works closely with each brand to create campaigns that make sense — both visually and strategically. Every creator is handpicked. Every piece of content is intentional. Every result is measured and improved.

  • Where We’re Headed

    We’re still growing — and we like it that way. Bravyn is a work in progress, always learning, always adapting. Our goal is to keep building long-term relationships, keep exploring new creative ground, and keep pushing influencer marketing to be more human, more thoughtful, and more impactful.

UrbanCore – Streetwear Meets City Culture

Launched UrbanCore’s new collection with a cultural edge and drove brand recognition among Gen Z.

Bravyn selected 15 niche fashion influencers from New York, Berlin, and London, each known for their unique street style. We crafted bold, noir-themed visuals with strong visual storytelling, including urban shoots, graffiti walls, and stylized slow-motion Reels. We used a mix of Instagram Reels, TikTok challenges, and story takeovers to amplify the release.

  • Reach: 2.1M+ across all platforms
  • Engagement Rate: 8.4% (2.5x industry avg.)
  • Brand IG follower growth: +17K in 2 weeks

Suné – Beauty in Motion

Created awareness for Suné’s new SPF skincare line and shifted perception from traditional to luxury-minimal.

We activated a mix of macro and micro beauty creators, each with a soft, cinematic content style. The campaign was built around the idea of “SPF as a ritual,” featuring creators applying the product during golden hour in highly aesthetic bathroom setups and outdoor scenes. All visuals maintained a grainy, pastel-toned filter to stay on-brand.

  • Video Views: 4.6M total
  • Conversion Rate from link in bio: 5.8%
  • 60+ UGC assets created
  • Featured on 3 viral TikTok roundups

TitanFit – Performance With A Pulse

Launched TitanFit’s newest smart wearable by tapping into fitness creators who inspire performance and lifestyle.

We ran a 3-week influencer series: “A Day With Titan,” showcasing creators’ fitness routines, biometric data, and lifestyle integrations. Content was split between hard-hitting gym edits and chill, everyday life scenes — highlighting the duality of the product. We also hosted a live challenge week with giveaways and branded hashtags.

  • Campaign Hashtag #TrainWithTitan: 9.2M views
  • 120K site visits during campaign window
  • Secured partnership with 2 new retail chains due to online demand
